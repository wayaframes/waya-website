The Challenge

Some founders are great at the technical side, some at operations, others at sales, and even some at fundraising and story. Very few are good at all of it — and if narrative and developing pitches aren't your strong suit, then you're never going to raise. All your other strengths will never get to shine without capital.

VCs invest in founders they believe in — founders who tell a story they fall in love with. It sounds very simple to do; it is not. We help do a deep dive on your company and build a narrative that will get investors excited — and money in the bank.

Process Overview & Steps

Process overview

A working session focused on your fundraising story. We dig into your company, your market, and your existing pitch, then build the narrative together — the version that makes investors believe. You leave able to tell it with conviction.

  • 01Pre-call: share your deck, current pitch, and what you're raising — so we understand your company and where the story stands today.
  • 02Book call: pick the time that works best for you.
  • 03Analysis: Na'ama digs into your company, market, and materials to find the throughline investors will care about.
  • 04Call: we work through different narrative angles till something pops.
  • 05Follow up: we tighten the theme that has the right angle, and it's yours to utilize in your pitch deck and beyond.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Waya

What is Fundraising Narrative?

It's a working session where we build the story you raise on. We dig into your company and market, work through narrative angles with you, then tighten the one that lands — yours to use in your pitch deck and every investor conversation.

What stage should I be at to book a Fundraising Narrative session?

If you're raising or plan to in the next 1–6 months, this is for you — from Seed through Series B+. Story matters at every stage.

What if I already have a pitch or deck?

Perfect — that's the starting point. We pressure-test what you've got, find the angle that actually lands, and sharpen it. Whether your story is rough or already polished, we make it better.

How is Fundraising Narrative different from Data Room Preparation?

Data Room Preparation organizes your materials. Fundraising Narrative is the story that ties them together.

Do we have to be a US-based company to work with you?

No, our system works for companies in any region.

Does my company have to be a tech startup?

Waya works with technology companies as well as physical-world companies. You can be the founder of a tech startup, a non-profit organization, or a physical-world business. We are particularly effective for organizations that have a high level of complexity, like supply chain, healthcare, and logistics.
