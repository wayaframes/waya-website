Audit Overview

One hour. One constraint. Three WayaFrames.

A quick diagnostic of your operations status, benchmarked against your goals and your competitors. The Operations Audit is a one-hour deep dive in which we diagnose your operations gap-to-goals and give you 3 AI-enabled WayaFrames to implement in your business.

  • 01Pre-call: collect company and constraint information to dig into your burning issues.
  • 02Book call: select the time that works best for you.
  • 03Analysis: Waya analyzes your details, puts together an interview script, and matches you with a consultant from our expert network — so our time is best spent.
  • 04Call: a conversation about your burning issues, what you have tried, and the systems that you have in place.
  • 05Follow up: receive an actionable report on key blockers and a WayaFrame toolkit to help solve them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Waya

What is an Operations Audit?

It is a one-hour deep dive session in which we discover the key constraint(s) holding back your business. At the end of the session, we send you an analysis and key action items to work on. We also provide 3 WayaFrames to help you start unblocking these constraints today.

What are WayaFrames?

WayaFrames are AI-enabled frameworks that live inside your Claude Teams or Claude Cowork environment. Think of them as giving your leadership team access to a seasoned operator on demand. Each WayaFrame was designed by an industry and operations expert, encoding decades of hard-won knowledge into a structured, guided flow that integrates with your company's documents, data, and business applications.

Do your WayaFrames work together?

WayaFrames work together as an operating system — each one connects to the others, to your business context and to your business applications. The more you use them, the better they get in helping your business run as a single, coherent system.

What is unique about the process?

We're not management consultants who hand over a strategy deck and disappear. We're founders and operators who design, plan, and execute alongside you — and every audit ends with tools you can deploy the same day.

What stage companies can you help?

We work with companies from the early stage through growth. The Operations Audit can be tailored to companies of all stages.

Do we have to be a US-based company to work with you?

No, our system works for companies in any region.

Does my company have to be a tech startup?

Waya works with technology companies as well as physical-world companies. You can be the founder of a tech startup, a non-profit organization, or a physical-world business. We are particularly effective for organizations with a high level of complexity, like supply chain, healthcare, and logistics.

When should I schedule a call?

When you've hit the ceiling of what you can solve alone. When you're the bottleneck and you know it. When you need a founder who's been in the trenches to help you navigate a critical transition — a scale-up, a pivot, the chaos in between.
