Process Overview

One Hour Session

A focused review of your data room — benchmarked against what investors at your stage actually expect to see. We go through your data room, financial documentation, legal foundation, and cap table together. After the call we fill in the gaps and give you the final data room for your raise.

  • 01Pre-call: collect company stage, fundraising history, and document inventory to focus on your specific gaps.

  • 03Analysis: Waya reviews your materials against the stage-appropriate data room checklist and matches you with an expert from our network.
  • 04Call: we walk through your data room gap-by-gap — what exists, what's missing, what's stale — then guide you on how to close the delta.
  • 05Follow up: we build and update your data room docs so it's fully ready to close your deal.

What Your Data Room Needs, By Stage

Every fundraising stage has different expectations. We calibrate your data room to what your next round demands.

Seed / Pre Series A

Story and foundations

It is all about the story and the problem you are solving. Focus: can you articulate a compelling narrative, and is everything legally in place.

Series A

Proof it scales

Investors are validating that product-market fit is proven and the business is ready to scale. Focus: full financial history, unit economics, customer concentration, and corporate governance.

Series B

Path to market leader

Given capital, how quickly can your company become the market leader? Focus: market dynamics, detailed cohort economics, audited financials, regulatory compliance, and full IP and contract portfolios.

Investors don't just run due diligence on your product. They run it on your organization.

A clean data room signals operational maturity. A messy one tells investors everything about how you run your company — before you've even pitched.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Waya

What is Data Room Preparation?

It's a one-hour working session where we review your data room against what investors expect at your current stage — across storytelling, financial documentation, data room structure, legal foundation, and cap table. After the call, we fill in the gaps and deliver the final data room for your raise.

What stage should I be at to book a Data Room Preparation session?

If you're planning to raise in the next 1–6 months — or if you just closed a round and want to maintain your data room so the next raise isn't another scramble — this is for you. We calibrate to your stage, from Seed through Series B+.

How is it different from Financial Model Preparation?

Data Room Preparation gets your full set of investor materials organized and complete. Financial Model Preparation goes deep on the piece investors scrutinize most — your model. The model we build drops straight into your data room.

What if I don't have a data room yet?

That's exactly when you should book a session. Starting to prepare your data room a few months before a raise is dramatically better than scrambling from scratch when an investor sends a DD request list while you're still pitching.

What stage companies can you help?

We work with companies from the early stage through growth. Our Data Room, Financial Model, and Fundraising Narrative sessions are tailored to companies of all stages.

Do we have to be a US-based company to work with you?

No, our system works for companies in any region.

Does my company have to be a tech startup?

Waya works with technology companies as well as physical-world companies. You can be the founder of a tech startup, a non-profit organization, or a physical-world business. We are particularly effective for organizations with a high level of complexity, like supply chain, healthcare, and logistics.
